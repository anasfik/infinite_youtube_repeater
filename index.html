FAQ

Infinity is a web app made for personal use but open to eveyone, it's useful when you want to loop a youtube video to infinity, or specify the number repeat times, it comes with ssome good settings specifications such starting from specific time in video, or skipping parts, you can also bookmark videos for future use without sign in, enjoy it :)

When you like some video/song, and you want just to listen to it in looped mode, or you want to play only a part of video without actually downloading it or playing it at some music streaming websites, you can bookmark a video you like it, and it still on your bookmarks page even if you reload the page on the same browser
